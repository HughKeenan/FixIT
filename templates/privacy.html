Our Privacy Policy

Who We Are:

FixIT is the controller of your personal data as described in this policy. If you have any questions, you can contact us at: privacy@fixit.com

Information We Collect:

i. Information provided by you:

  • Account information (name, email, password)
  • Your queries
  • Feedback, support requests and bug reports

ii. Information collected automatically:

  • Log data (e.g., IP address, browser type, date/time)
  • Device information
  • Usage data (e.g., interactions with our services)

iii. Information from Third Parties

We may receive data from service providers or business partners as permitted by law.

How we use your information:

We may use your info to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Communicate with you
  • Ensure security and prevent abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Conduct research and development

Where appropriate, we may rely on the following legal bases:

  • Performance of a contract
  • Legitimate interests
  • Compliance with legal obligations
  • Consent (where applicable)

International Data Transfers

Your personal data may be transferred to countries outside of the EEA, UK, or Switzerland. When we do, we ensure appropriate safeguards, such as Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission.

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Correct or update inaccurate information
  • Delete your personal data
  • Object to or restrict processing
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent (where applicable)
  • Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority

To exercise your rights, contact us at privacy@fixit.com.

Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

Children

FixIT’s services are not intended for children under the age of 13 (or as required by local law). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children without parental consent.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We’ll notify you of significant changes via email or through our services.
